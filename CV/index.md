## Skills

* Coding experience in R, Python, and Julia  
* Bioinformatics: targeted genome assembly, whole genome assembly, variant calling, whole-genome alignment, ortholog identification, selection analyses, GWAS.  
* Experience working with both short-read and long-read genomes.  
* Experience in inferring phylogenies with genomic data.  
* µ-Computer-tomography scanning of skeletal material of museum specimens.  
* Reconstruction, post-processing, volume rendering, and segmentation of µCT-scan data in VGStudioMax, GeoMagic, and 3D Slicer.  
* Digital Curation of Specimens: data entry in Specify, geo-referencing, updating taxonomy, and clean-up of historical data.  
* Preparation of physical specimens: wet (formalin-fixed) and dry (skeletons and skins).  
* Preparation and management of inter-institution loans.  
* Molecular lab techniques: DNA extraction, polymerase chain reaction, gel electrophoresis, environmental DNA extraction, RNA extraction, and genomic library preparation.  
* Preparation and curation of tissue material.  
* Collection of different tissue types in the field, including: muscle, skin, liver, kidney, lungs, heart, gonads, and blood.  
* Phylogenetic tree inference using IQTree-2, BEAST2, RaxML, ASTRAL-III, and SVDQuartets.  
* Ecological niche modeling and species distribution modeling in MaxEnt.
